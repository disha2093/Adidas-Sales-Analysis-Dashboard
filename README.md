# 📊 Adidas Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI  

## 🚀 Project Overview  
This project presents an interactive **Adidas Sales Dashboard** built using Power BI. It focuses on analyzing sales performance, profit trends, and business insights across regions, products, and retailers.  

The dashboard transforms raw data into actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.  

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance  
- Identify top-performing regions and products  
- Track seasonal trends and sales patterns  
- Evaluate retailer contribution to revenue  

---

## 📈 Key Metrics  
- 💰 **Total Sales:** $899.9M  
- 📊 **Total Profit:** $332.1M  
- 📦 **Units Sold:** 2.5M  
- 💵 **Average Price per Unit:** $45.2  
- 📉 **Average Margin:** 42.3%  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features  

### 📅 Sales Trends  
- Monthly sales performance visualization  
- Peak sales observed during **July–August**  
- Clear seasonal patterns in revenue  

### 🌍 Regional Analysis  
- Sales distribution across regions:  
  *West, Northeast, Southeast, South, Midwest*  
- Identifies high-performing and low-performing regions  

### 👟 Product Insights  
- Top-performing categories:  
  - Men’s Street Footwear  
  - Women’s Apparel  
- Enables product-level performance tracking  

### 🏬 Retailer Performance  
- Comparative analysis of key retailers:  
  - West Gear  
  - Foot Locker  
  - Sports Direct  
- Highlights major contributors to overall revenue  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard development  
- **Data Visualization** – Interactive charts & KPIs

## 🚀 Key Insights  
- Strong **seasonal trends** impact overall sales  
- A few regions contribute the majority of revenue  
- Top product categories drive significant sales volume  
- Limited retailers account for a large share of total sales
